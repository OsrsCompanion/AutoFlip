What the website gives you — and what it intentionally does not

This should answer the landing-page question fast: the free website is for discovery, trust-building, and partial planning. Auto Flip Pro is for full 8-slot workflow, exact board deployment, and guided execution inside RuneLite.

Website: free discovery layer

  • Explorer + charts: compare items, spread, trend, and liquidity context.
  • Favorites + watchlist: keep a short list worth revisiting.
  • Preview-only AI: enough to guide thinking, not enough to fully run the board.
  • Partial plans: prove value without replacing the plugin.

Auto Flip Pro: premium execution layer

  • Full 8-slot optimization: stronger coverage than the preview experience.
  • Exact quantities: clearer slot-by-slot deployment.
  • Budget-aware planning: your budget, timing, and risk shape the board.
  • RuneLite workflow: continue execution where the player is already trading.
