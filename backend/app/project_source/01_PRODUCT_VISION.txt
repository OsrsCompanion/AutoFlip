AUTOFLIP.GG — PRODUCT VISION

CORE IDEA
AutoFlip is a board-first, guided execution engine for OSRS flipping.
It is not a data site and not a margin checker.
It reads the player’s situation, builds the best GE board it can, and tells the player exactly what to do next.

PRIMARY AUDIENCE
1) Primary: players who want GP but do not want to learn flipping.
2) Secondary: experienced flippers who want to keep most of their results while spending far less time.

CORE PROMISE
- Spend less time flipping. More time playing.
- Make almost as much money as expert flippers without doing all the work.
- Fill your GE faster with less thinking.

NORTH STAR
A member account opens the GE, clicks Optimize, and is guided to a strong 8-slot board with simple explanations and clear next steps.

WHAT WE ARE BUILDING
AutoFlip manages the full board, not just item picks.
It should answer:
- What should I buy?
- What should I replace?
- What should I keep?
- What should I sell now?
- What should I do next?

PRODUCT DOCTRINE
- Beginner-first experience, expert-capable backend.
- Action-first UI.
- Board-native, not item-native.
- Explain every important action in simple words a 15-year-old can understand.
- Optimize for time saved, not theoretical max profit.
- Website gives evidence and desire; plugin completes the board.

FREE VS PAID BOUNDARY
Website:
- discovery
- trust building
- partial value
- preview / shortlist / conviction
- intentionally incomplete

Plugin:
- full 8-slot optimization
- exact price + quantity guidance
- replace / hold / cancel logic
- guided execution inside RuneLite
- future recovery workflow

KEY DIFFERENTIATOR
Competitors mostly stop at data, filters, and lists.
AutoFlip owns the execution layer:
- board construction
- slot-by-slot runbooks
- replace/cancel guidance
- explainable action sequencing

COMMUNICATION RULE
Explain like the user is 15, but not stupid.
Bad: “liquidity inefficiency due to spread instability”
Good: “This item is too slow, so your money gets stuck.”

TRUST RULES
- No automation of gameplay.
- No generated clicks or keypresses.
- User places every offer.
- AutoFlip guides, highlights, suggests, and explains.
- Trust is part of the product, not an afterthought.
