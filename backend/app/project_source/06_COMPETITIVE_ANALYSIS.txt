AUTOFLIP.GG — COMPETITIVE ANALYSIS

MARKET TRUTH
The OSRS flipping market is saturated with data tools but still under-serves decision execution.
Most tools show margins, charts, filters, and alerts.
Few tools answer: what do I do next, with this board, right now?

WHAT COMPETITORS GENERALLY DO WELL
- charts and watchlists
- margin visibility
- profit tracking
- alerts
- some in-client convenience

WHAT THEY USUALLY DO NOT SOLVE
- fill probability in a user-friendly way
- exact next-step guidance
- board-level capital allocation
- slot-by-slot replacement logic
- trustworthy explanations for cancel / rotate / wait decisions

KEY USER PAINS OBSERVED
- stale or bait margins
- no fill-time confidence
- execution ambiguity (price / qty / cancel rule)
- analysis paralysis
- trust issues around accuracy and latency
- fear of crossing the automation / bannable boundary

STRATEGIC TAKEAWAYS
1. Data is no longer the moat.
2. Execution guidance is the moat.
3. Trust must be visible.
4. Board-native UX beats item-native UX.
5. Time saved is a stronger sales pitch than theoretical max profit.

POSITIONING
Competitors mostly say:
- here is what looks good
- here are the numbers

AutoFlip should say:
- here is your board
- here is your next move
- here is why

DEFENSIBLE ADVANTAGE
AutoFlip’s edge is not more research.
It is an explainable, action-first, 8-slot operating system for trading capital.
