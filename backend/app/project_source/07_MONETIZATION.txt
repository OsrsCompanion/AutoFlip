AUTOFLIP.GG — MONETIZATION

BUSINESS MODEL
Free website + paid plugin.

FREE WEBSITE SHOULD PROVIDE
- discovery
- shortlist / conviction
- partial board preview
- market explorer
- favorites / watch features
- evidence that AutoFlip knows what it is doing

FREE WEBSITE MUST NOT PROVIDE
- full 8-slot optimization
- full execution guidance
- plugin-level workflow speed
- complete replacement logic
- full capital deployment

PAID PLUGIN SHOULD PROVIDE
- full board optimizer
- exact quantities and prices
- runbook per slot
- replace / cancel / collect / wait guidance
- player-aware board management
- future recovery workflow

PRIMARY VALUE SOLD
Time compression.
Users are not paying for raw data.
They are paying to make good money without doing all the work.

PRIMARY SALES PITCHES
- Fill your GE faster.
- Make almost as much as expert flippers in a tiny fraction of the time.
- Stop guessing what to buy and sell.

AUDIENCE SEGMENTS
1. Beginner / non-flipper
- wants GP
- does not want to learn flipping
- needs confidence and simplicity

2. Existing flipper
- values time saved
- willing to trade a little edge for huge convenience

PRICING LOGIC
Price should feel like paying for speed, clarity, and reduced mistakes.
Complex pricing tiers are less important than a sharp free-to-plugin boundary.
